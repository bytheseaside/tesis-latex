\subsubsection{Maxwell’s Equations as a General Physical Framework}
The propagation of electrical signals in biological tissue is governed by the fundamental laws of electromagnetism, formally described by Maxwell’s equations. These equations provide a unified framework that relates electric fields, charge distributions, and material properties across space and time. Although originally formulated for a wide range of physical phenomena, they also form the theoretical basis for modeling bioelectric signal propagation in conductive media.
