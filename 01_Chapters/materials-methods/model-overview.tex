% Model Overview
%-----------------------------------------------------------

The mannequin geometry was processed to create a shell with a uniform wall thickness of 
\nota{FINAL THICKNESS HERE, approx\ 3.2~mm} \nota{in the areas corresponding to the 21 probe locations for the EEG measuring international 10/20 system}. 
This shell was divided into two interlocking halves (superior and inferior) to simplify printing, 
assembly, and internal inspection.

The internal cavity was designed to be filled with a gel-like conductive medium occupying the region 
corresponding to the cranial volume. 
This space establishes the physical domain where the internal antennas and wire guides are embedded, 
allowing the propagation of electrical fields to be studied under controlled conditions 
(see Section~\ref{sec:conductive_medium}).

Dedicated openings were integrated into the lid to facilitate gel pouring and the alignment 
of the internal wire-guides.
